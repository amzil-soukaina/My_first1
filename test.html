The Solar System

The solar system is a collection of planets and celestial bodies that orbit around the sun. It is where we all live.

Learn more about the solar system

Stages of Solar System Formation:

  1. Formation of the initial gas cloud.
  2. Matter fusion and the creation of the sun.
  3. Planets forming around the sun.

Facts about the Solar System:

  • It consists of 8 planets.
  • The sun is the central star.
  • It includes planets, asteroids, and comets.

Definitions related to the Solar System:

Sun:
The central star of the solar system
Planets:
Bodies that orbit the sun, like Earth and Mars
Asteroids:
Small rocky objects orbiting the sun
